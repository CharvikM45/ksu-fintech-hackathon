Vision & Roadmap

Where We're Going.

From a $35 Raspberry Pi to a nationwide decentralized financial network. Here's our path from prototype to ubiquitous infrastructure across Uganda and beyond.

Phase 1 — Concept & Research Completed Q1 2026

  • Market analysis of unbanked populations in emerging markets
  • Cryptography research for offline double-spend prevention
  • Initial hardware prototyping and component sourcing

Phase 2 — Prototype & Development Current

  • Build end-to-end mesh network using LoRa/Bluetooth
  • Develop Flask-based centralized ledger and reconciliation engine
  • Finalize CAD models for the merchant terminal
  • Launch internal alpha test with simulated offline conditions

Phase 3 — Testing & Validation Q3 2026

  • Third-party security audit of the offline signing protocol
  • Hardware stress testing (thermal, power failure, connectivity loss)
  • UI/UX studies with actual merchants and users

Phase 4 — Pilot Launch Q4 2026

  • Setup Raspberry Pi systems in local, rural areas across Uganda
  • Partner with Raspberry Pi Foundation for hardware stock and donations
  • Measure % of fraudulent transactions detected, daily active users, and offline transaction rate

Phase 5 — Scale & Expand 2027

  • Deploy Raspberry Pi nodes across Uganda at scale
  • Referral incentives for users and merchants — community-led adoption model
  • Small transaction fee for sustainable revenue and microfinance partnerships for lending
Expected Impact
1,000+ users protected in the first year — bringing secure, offline-first banking to communities that traditional infrastructure has left behind.
$25,000+ in estimated fraud savings through on-device AI detection — Wall Street-level analytics on a $35 computer.
40%+ increase in user confidence and community trust. When the network goes down, their economy doesn't.
