Where We're Going.

Our mission is to establish Bankify as the default offline financial rail globally. Here is our master plan to scale from prototype to ubiquitous infrastructure.

Phase 1 — Concept & Research Completed Q1 2026

  • Market analysis of unbanked populations in emerging markets
  • Cryptography research for offline double-spend prevention
  • Initial hardware prototyping and component sourcing

Phase 2 — Prototype & Development Current

  • Build end-to-end mesh network using LoRa/Bluetooth
  • Develop Flask-based centralized ledger and reconciliation engine
  • Finalize CAD models for the merchant terminal
  • Launch internal alpha test with simulated offline conditions

Phase 3 — Testing & Validation Q3 2026

  • Third-party security audit of the offline signing protocol
  • Hardware stress testing (thermal, power failure, connectivity loss)
  • UI/UX studies with actual merchants and users

Phase 4 — Partnerships & Pilot Launch Q4 2026

  • Secure partnerships with local NGOs and regional banks
  • Deploy 100 beta terminals in a target pilot region
  • Monitor transaction throughput and reconciliation speeds

Phase 5 — Full Market Launch 2027

  • Scale production of Bankify terminals
  • Open API platform for third-party developer integration
  • Expand to 5 new target countries
